About SIADD

The Smart Industry Anomaly Detection Dataset (SIADD) is a specialized dataset designed for Cyber-Physical Threat Monitoring in Industrial IoT Environments.

In modern smart industries, cyber-physical systems integrate physical processes with networked computing. This convergence increases vulnerability to sophisticated attacks that can disrupt operations, compromise safety, and cause significant financial loss.

SIADD provides a realistic, labeled dataset to train machine learning models for detecting anomalies and attacks in real-time, enabling proactive defense and system resilience.

Our Vision

To build intelligent, resilient industrial systems that detect and respond to cyber threats before they cause harm.

Our Mission

Enable real-time anomaly detection in Industrial IoT using advanced machine learning to protect critical infrastructure.
